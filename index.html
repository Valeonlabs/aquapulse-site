Designed to reduce uncertainty

AquaPulse is not just a logbook. It is being built as an intelligent aquarium assistant that helps fishkeepers understand what is happening in their tank and make more confident care decisions.

AquaPulse provides informational guidance only. It is not a veterinary service and should not replace expert, aquatic health or professional specialist advice for serious livestock health issues.

Built by fishkeepers, for everyday hobbyists.

AquaPulse was created to solve a simple problem: most aquarium apps focus on recording data, while fishkeepers often need help understanding what that data means.


Our goal is to help aquarium keepers make better decisions, protect their livestock and enjoy the hobby with greater confidence.
