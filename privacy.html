Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AquaPulse collects, uses and protects information when you use the AquaPulse mobile app and related website.

AquaPulse is an aquarium care and guidance app. It provides informational guidance only and is not a veterinary, medical or professional livestock health service.

1. Who we are

AquaPulse is operated by Valeon Labs. For privacy or support questions, contact us at valeonlabs@proton.me.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use AquaPulse, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as email address or sign-in provider details.
  • Aquarium information, such as tank names, tank type, size, livestock, plants, equipment and setup details.
  • Water parameter logs, maintenance records, reminders and aquarium care history.
  • App activity information, such as feature usage, app interactions and diagnostic events.
  • Device or technical information, such as device identifiers, crash logs, operating system, app version and security/app integrity signals.
  • Purchase and subscription status, where relevant, processed through Google Play and RevenueCat.
  • Photos or image uploads, if you choose to use image-based features in the app.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide aquarium tracking, tank management and guidance features.
  • Generate AI-assisted aquarium care explanations and insights.
  • Save your tanks, logs, reminders and account preferences.
  • Manage free and premium feature access.
  • Improve app reliability, security and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal, security and platform requirements.

4. AI-assisted features

AquaPulse may use cloud-based AI services to generate aquarium guidance based on information you provide, such as tank details, water readings, livestock information or questions you ask. Do not submit sensitive personal information in AI prompts or aquarium notes.

5. Third-party services

AquaPulse may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase and Google Cloud services for authentication, database, cloud functions, storage, security and app infrastructure.
  • Google Play services for app distribution, billing and subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management.
  • AI service providers used to process aquarium guidance requests.

These services may process information as required to provide their functionality. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information only where necessary to operate the app, process subscriptions, provide cloud or AI features, comply with legal obligations, investigate abuse, or protect the security and integrity of AquaPulse.

7. Data storage and security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. Data transmitted between the app and cloud services is encrypted in transit. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Account deletion and data deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting valeonlabs@proton.me. Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, billing or compliance reasons.

9. Children’s privacy

AquaPulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Your choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, but some app features may not work without it. You can also uninstall the app at any time to stop further app-based data collection.

11. International processing

Information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our service providers operate. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used for international data processing.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

13. Contact

For privacy questions, support requests or data deletion requests, contact: valeonlabs@proton.me.
